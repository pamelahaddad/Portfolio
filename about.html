Myself In Short

Who I am & What I Do

Hello and thank you for visiting Pamela Haddad’s portfolio. I am her, and she is me, if the name didn’t already make it clear.So yeah my name is Pamela Haddad, I’m a Lebanese painter and designer residing in Madrid, and I’m specialized in graphic design, UX/UI design, web design, and development.I’m currently seeking to get my Master’s in UX/UI. Additionally, I have a Master in Digital Marketing and a Bachelor of Arts in Interior Design. My passion for the design sector first sparked when I started building my digital design and marketing agency, Posh Design, which you will get to know more about in the coming section below. Here’s a fun fact about me, I am fluent in four languages, English, Spanish, French and Arabic. As you might notice in some of my works, I love using these languages to play with typography and visuals.

What Is Posh Design?

Posh Design is an online agency that offers both design and marketing services. Initially, it began by building Posh Canvas, an online gallery where I displayed my art designs and paintings. A year later, I partnered with my sister Karen to build a sister company that prioritizes design and marketing, and that was when Posh design was born. At Posh, our main goal is to meet our clients’ unique requirements by delivering top-notch design and marketing solutions.
