Selected Work

Lebanese Artists Box

This book was initially designed and created as a final project for an Editoril Graphic Design course. Its goal is to emphasize the rich heritage of Lebanese art, the outstanding potential of emerging designers, and the limitless imagination of Lebanese artists.It stands out because it’s an entire collection rather than just a book! It includes a selection of postcards, flyers, and posters in various sizes, all organized concisely in one box. And being a proud Lebanese artist, I made sure to incorporate both Arabic and English within the design.

Coffice Coffee Shop

As part of a Graphic Design Editorial course, I took on a branding project for Coffice coffee shop. Specifically, I developed two unique illustrations for the coffee shop’s new branding campaign during the Halloween season. Each illustration was paired with a catchy slogan, “Hocus Pocus I Need Coffee to Focus” and “More Espresso less Depresso,” with the designs and colors seamlessly integrated to match the overall theme.
